Students need to be aware of the following policies:

  1. Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)

    1

    • Available to people aged 18 to 70 years with a bank account.
    • Provides coverage for accidental death and disability.
    • Premium of Rs. 20 per annum deducted from the bank account.

  2. Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

    2

    • Provides guaranteed minimum monthly pension to subscribers.
    • Open to saving bank/post office saving bank account holders aged 18 to 40 years.

  3. Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)

    3

    • Ensures comprehensive financial inclusion by providing universal access to banking facilities.
    • Allows opening of savings accounts without the requirement of any minimum balance.

These are some of the policies that students should be aware of. It is important to note that there may be other policies as well, depending on the specific needs and circumstances of the students.
